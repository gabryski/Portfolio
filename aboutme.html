Who is G.Sobolewski?

Gabrielle is a dedicated student expected to graduate in May 2018 with Honors from the University of Massachusetts Amherst. She is double-majoring in English and Human Ecology as well as pursuing a certificate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication.

Ms. Sobolewski relishes spotless grammar, clever logic puzzles, and mugs full of locally roasted coffee. She is educated in HTML5 and CSS, Adobe Creative Suite, Adobe FrameMaker, Adobe Captivate, MadCap Flare, Microsoft Office, Axure RP, and the vast majority of social media platforms. She has experience working in a small business environment (Bulbs.com) as well as global corporations (Oracle).

Gabrielle's passions include problem-solving, writing, editing, UX design/research, and saving the the Oxford comma. Her vast experience extends to customer service, marketing, professional writing, non-profit organizational work, editing, and translating for a Polish-American parish bulletin. She hopes to further her experience in a junior position in one of the above fields.
